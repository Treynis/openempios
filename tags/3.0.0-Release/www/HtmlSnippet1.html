OpenEMPI Architecture - Version 2.0  

 

After the redesign and refactoring of OpenEMPI, the architecture of the software is now based on the principles of Service Oriented Architecture consisting of a number of independent services that are loosely coupled from the rest. Each service is exposed to other services through a well defined interface. Figure 1 illustrates the high level architecture of OpenEMPI bringing out the multi-layered architecture of the software. The diagram of Figure 1 is not an exhaustive list of all the components that comprise the system but only shows a subset of them for the purpose of illustrating the layered architecture of the system and to bring out the loose coupling between the services.
